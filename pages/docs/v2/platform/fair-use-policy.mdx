## Usage Guidelines

As a guideline for our community, we expect most users to fall within the below ranges for each plan. We will notify you if your usage is an outlier. Our goal is to be as permissive as possible while not allowing an unreasonable burden on our infrastructure. Where possible, we'll reach out to you ahead of any action we take to address unreasonable usage and work with you to correct it.

### Typical Monthly Usage Guidelines

<br />

|                                 | Hobby           | Pro               |
| ------------------------------- | --------------- | ----------------- |
| Bandwidth                       | Up to 1TB       | Up to 100GB       |
| Serverless Function Invocations | Up to 100GB/Hrs | Up to 1,000GB/Hrs |
| Builds per Week                 | Up to 25        | Up to 100         |

### General Limits

To prevent abuse of our platform, we apply the following limits to all accounts.

<br />

|                                             | Hobby        | Pro          |
| ------------------------------------------- | ------------ | ------------ |
| Maximum Serverless Functions per Deployment | 12 Functions | 64 Functions |
| Maximum Serverless Function Duration        | 3 Seconds    | 5 Seconds    |
| Maximum CLI Deploys                         | 25           | 25           |

<Note>
  Next.js Static Site Generation and Preview functions are excluded from the
  count for "Maximum Functions per Deployment".
</Note>

For further information regarding this policy and/or acceptable use of our Services, please refer to our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms) or your Enterprise Service Agreement.
